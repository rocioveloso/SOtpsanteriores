
-----------EJEMPLOS DE SUSCRIPCION---------
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR NEW_POKEMON 123
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR APPEARED_POKEMON 123
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR CATCH_POKEMON 123
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR CAUGHT_POKEMON 123
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR GET_POKEMON 123
./gameboy BROKER SUSCRIPTOR LOCALIZED_POKEMON 123

--------------MENSAJES A BROKER------------
./gameboy BROKER NEW_POKEMON PIKACHU 1 2 3
./gameboy BROKER APPEARED_POKEMON PIKACHU 1 2 3
./gameboy BROKER CATCH_POKEMON PIKACHU 1 2
./gameboy BROKER CAUGHT_POKEMON 1 OK
./gameboy BROKER GET_POKEMON PIKACHU
./gameboy BROKER LOCALIZED_POKEMON 1 PIKACHU 2 1 1 2 2

--------------MENSAJES A GAMECARD------------

Todos los mensajes de cameCard tienen el id al final, ponemos un 0 como ejemplo.

./gameboy GAMECARD NEW_POKEMON PIKACHU 1 2 3 0
./gameboy GAMECARD CATCH_POKEMON PIKACHU 1 2 0
./gameboy GAMECARD GET_POKEMON PIKACHU 0
